# Payment Models Overview: phmedtecheu G-LMS Development

This document serves as an educational guide to help the Client choose the financial model that best aligns with their business goals and cash flow capacity for **phmedtecheu**.

---

## **Model A: The CapEx / OpEx Model (Asset Ownership)**

### **What is it?**
This model treats the German Learning App as a **Capital Asset**. You invest upfront to build the product, and once it is "built," you own it as a business asset.

**CapEx (Capital Expenditure)**: A one-time investment to build the core engine, gamification, and infrastructure.  
**OpEx (Operating Expenditure)**: The ongoing, predictable costs to keep the app "running" on the internet (technical maintenance and monitoring).

### **Financial Breakdown**
*   **Initial Build**: **₱40,000 / €655** per major roadmap milestone.
*   **Monthly Operations**: **₱2,500 / €41** (Fixed Technical Maintenance).
*   **Infrastructure**: Direct reimbursement of hosting and domain costs at cost.

---

## **Model B: The Partnership Model (Retainer & Scaling)**

### **What is it?**
This model treats the Developer as a **Long-term Partner** and scales your costs based on your actual business success. You pay significantly less upfront, but the Developer shares in the rewards of the app's growth.

**1. Sign-on & Stabilization**: A reduced, one-time fee to cover the initial technical setup and launch.  
**2. Support Retainer**: A fixed monthly "Subscription" for the Developer's priority availability and bug fixes.  
**3. Growth Royalty**: A flat "Success Fee" per active student enrollment in language courses.

### **Financial Breakdown**
*   **Setup Fee**: **₱15,000 / €245** (One-time).
*   **Monthly Retainer**: **₱5,000 / €82**.
*   **Success Royalty**: **₱350 / €6 per student** (Language Courses only).

---

## **Summary Comparison**

| Feature | Model A (Asset Ownership) | Model B (Partnership Model) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Build Cost**| **₱10k–₱40k / €165–€655** (Milestone) | **₱15,000 / €245** (Setup) |
| **Monthly Budget**    | **₱2,500 / €41** (Fixed) | **₱5,000 / €82** (Retainer) |
| **Growth Fee**        | **None** | **₱350 / €6** (Per Student) |
| **Risk Profile**      | Client takes initial risk | Shared risk / Shared reward |
| **Ownership Feel**    | Full Asset Ownership | Long-term Service Partnership |

---

## **Unity of Agreement**
Both models operate under the **Project Development Contract (PDC)** and the **Service Agreement & Fee Structure (SAFS)**. Any adjustment to these models requires mutual written consent from both parties.
