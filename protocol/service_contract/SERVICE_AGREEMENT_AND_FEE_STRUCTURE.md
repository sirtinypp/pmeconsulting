## 1. Executive Summary & Project Status
This document establishes the financial and operational framework for **phmedtecheu**. It operates as a sister-document to the **Project Development Contract (PDC)**, focusing specifically on fee structures, settlements, and infrastructure management.

**Nature of the Application**:  
**phmedtecheu** is a specialized, high-fidelity German Learning Management System (G-LMS) engineered for healthcare workers aspiring to work in Europe. Unlike general language apps, it integrates medical-specific vocabulary, real-life clinical scenarios, and a gamified progression engine to maximize certification success. It serves as both a pedagogical tool and a career-mapping platform for medical professionals transitionining to the European healthcare sector.
