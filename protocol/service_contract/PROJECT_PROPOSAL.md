## **1. Executive Summary**
This proposal outlines the professional development of a **Custom Gamified Learning Management System (G-LMS)** designed specifically for healthcare workers preparing for German certification (A1 to C1). The system is built on a high-fidelity Django engine, providing a premium education experience that matches the high-value nature of your curriculum.

---

## **2. System Classification & Features**
The platform is classified as a **High-Performance G-LMS**, featuring:
*   **Structured Learning**: Dedicated tracks for A1-C1 with video, document, and quiz support.
*   **Engagement Engine**: Custom gamification (Points and Leveling) to ensure student retention.
*   **Asset Management**: Centralized storage for healthcare-specific lessons and resources.
*   **Professional UI**: A premium, "WOW-factor" design using modern web standards.

---

## **3. Implementation Roadmap**
The project is divided into four critical milestones:
1.  **Foundation**: Secure User Authentication & Database Architecture.
2.  **Learning Core**: Content delivery engine for Lessons, Videos, and PDFs.
3.  **Gamification Layer**: Progression tracking, points, and user achievements.
4.  **Final Launch Verification**: Data seeding (12 modules) and final system hardening.

---

## **4. Investment Options**
To align with your business capacity and cash flow goals, two distinct financial models are offered.

### **Option 1: The "Asset Ownership" Model (CapEx / OpEx)**
*Focus: Professional investment in a proprietary business asset.*
*   **Setup/Build (CapEx)**: **₱40,000** per Milestone (4 Milestones total).
*   **Maintenance (OpEx)**: **₱2,500 / month** flat fee for infrastructure integrity.
*   **Hosting**: Client reimburses direct server costs (Railway/Domain) at cost.

### **Option 2: The "Partnership" Model (Retainer / Royalty)**
*Focus: Shared risk and reward; scales as your business grows.*
*   **Initial Setup**: **₱15,000** (Covers initial deployment and core launch).
*   **Support Retainer**: **₱5,000 / month** (Priority availability for bug fixes and support).
*   **Growth Royalty**: **5% of Student Enrollment Fee** (Approx. ₱600–₱1,750 per student).
*   **Hosting**: Client reimburses direct server costs (Railway/Domain) at cost.
