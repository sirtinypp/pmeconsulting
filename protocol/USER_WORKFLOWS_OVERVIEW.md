# User Journey & Workflow Specifications: PME Learning Platform

**Standard Operating Procedures for Guest, Student, and Admin Interactions**

---

## 1. The Guest Visitor Lifecycle
The **Guest** workflow is engineered to maximize conversion by providing a low-friction entry point while strictly protecting premium intellectual property.

### **Phase 1: Discovery**
*   **Landing Page Exploration**: Visitors view the high-fidelity hero section, medical impact statistics, and our unique clinical German vision.
*   **Public Catalog**: Guests can browse the structure of the A1–C1 courses and the live training calendar to see the active community.

### **Phase 2: Lead Capture (Registration)**
*   **Zero-Cost Signup**: Visitors create a `GUEST` account in seconds. This converts a "viewer" into a "known user" for future marketing and service inquiries.
*   **Initial Dashboard**: Access is restricted to a simplified "Preview Dashboard" with a clear pathway to upgrade.

### **Phase 3: Conversion Anchor**
*   **Access Lock**: Attempting to enroll in a course or view clinical videos triggers the **Upgrade Requirement**.
*   **Upgrade Gateway**: Guests are presented with the value proposition of the Paid Student status (Full A1-C1 curriculum, clinical scenarios, etc.).

---

## 2. The Paid Student Workflow (Premium Experience)
Once the Guest transitions to a **Paid Student**, the platform unlocks its full G-LMS (Gamified LMS) power.

### **Academic Engagement**
*   **Course Enrollment**: Students can self-enroll in any specialized German module. These requests are sent to the Admin for final approval to ensure cohort quality.
*   **Module Completion**: Students progress through lessons, marking them complete to earn XP (Experience Points) and move through academic levels.

### **Resources & Clinical Simulation**
*   **Premium Materials**: Full access to high-fidelity videos, Hospital Scenario PDFs, and Medical Vocabulary lists.
*   **Quizzes & Assessment**: Required quizzes per module to unlock subsequent clinical advanced levels.

### **Interactive Learning**
*   **Speaking Sessions**: Direct access to the video links for weekly live instructor-led clinical workshops.
*   **Achievements**: Automated badges and certificates of completion recognized by the PME community.

---

## 3. The School Admin Workflow (Command Center)
Designed for **Christine Anne Gumboc (Subject Matter Expert)**, the Admin workflow provides 360-degree governance over the platform.

### **Operational Governance**
*   **Enrollment Verification**: Approve or deny pending course enrollment requests from students.
*   **Content Deployment**: Native "Command Center" to upload new video modules, clinical documents, and update the global training schedule.

### **Career Service Fulfillment**
*   **Lead Management**: Dashboard to track "Service Inquiries" (CV Reviews, Consultations, and European Readiness Checklists) from both guests and students.
*   **Manual Upgrades**: Ability to manually promote users from Guest to Student status for alternative payment methods (e.g., GCash/Bank Transfer).

### **Analytics & Impact**
*   **Student Tracking**: Monitor individual progress through the CEFR levels (Beginner A1 to Mastery C2).
*   **System Integrity**: Ensure all multi-tenant "School-Scoped" data remains secure and isolated.
